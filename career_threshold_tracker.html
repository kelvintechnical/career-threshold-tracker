Best geography for a comp-bio / LDE PhD (from Kinston, NC)

  • Research Triangle, NC (~1–1.5 hr) — Usually the strongest fit while staying regional: Duke, UNC Chapel Hill, NCSU — dense genomics, structural biology, ML-for-proteins, and directed-evolution adjacent groups.
  • Charlotte / Wake Forest / broader NC — Worth a targeted search if a specific PI matches LDE + your methods stack; fewer default options than the Triangle.
  • Atlanta (~6 hr) — Emory, Georgia Tech, CDC-adjacent work — good if you expand the Southeast radius.
  • National research hubs (relocation) — Boston/Cambridge (Broad, MIT, Harvard); SF Bay Area (Stanford, UCSF, Berkeley); San Diego (UCSD, Salk); Seattle (UW, Fred Hutch); DC–Maryland (NIH, JHU, UMD) — maximize advisor match for latent models + protein/engineering biology.

Salary bands in this tracker assume industry-style total comp for the US; graduate stipends are typically much lower (~$35K–$55K depending on program/cost of living). Post-PhD comp in biotech/pharma/tech can align with the upper bands when DL + domain depth match.
